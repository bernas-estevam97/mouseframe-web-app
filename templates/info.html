Information on mice walking behaviour analysis

The front paws of the mice are marked in red and the hind paws in blue. This analysis goes through 8 different types of measurements that are all manually calculated using a ruler in the laboratory. The 8 measures are as follows

  • Stride length left front
  • Stride length left hind
  • Stride length right front
  • Stride length right hind
  • Overlap Left
  • Overlap Right
  • Stride Width Front
  • Stride Width Hind
