Welcome to MouseFrame

The Digital Upgrade for the Inked Paw Test

Say goodbye to rulers and manual calculations. MouseFrame streamlines your behavioral research by automating footprint measurements from digitized sheets.

📄 1. Digitize

Upload your scanned footprint sheets directly into the application. Keep your records digital and easily accessible.

🎯 2. Mark Paws

Use our streamlined interface to correctly identify and mark paw placements on your uploaded sheets to ensure accurate data tracking.

📏 3. Automate

Once marked, MouseFrame automatically calculates all spatial metrics and distances between paws instantly, saving you hours of tedious lab work.
